Hikking and Tourism

Famous Marian city in the south-east of the Massif Central, starting point of the Pilgrimage to Santiago de Compostela, Le Puy-en-Velay is above all an exceptional site.

 


You will discover :

- its Cathedral, listed as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO
- its 12th century cloister
- The Statue of Notre Dame de France
- the rock of the Chapel St Michel, ...
